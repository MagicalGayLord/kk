Galaxy

Launched on January 22, 2025, the series includes the Galaxy S25, S25+, S25 Ultra, and later the ultra‑thin S25 Edge in May 2025

Design and Displays

All models sport 120 Hz Dynamic LTPO AMOLED 2X panels with up to 2,600 nits peak brightness. The S25 (6.2″) and S25+ (6.7″) have Gorilla Glass Victus 2, while the Ultra uses Gorilla Glass Armor 2. The Edge is the slimmest at ~6.4 mm

price

In the US, base S25 starts at $799, S25+ at $999, and S25 Ultra at $1,299. Preorders began Jan 2025, with general release on Feb 7. A slim Edge model debuted via carriers in May
